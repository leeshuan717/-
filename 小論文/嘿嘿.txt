2. 相關經典文獻
這些是一些與閥值處理與圖像分割相關的經典論文與書籍：

Otsu, N. (1979). "A Threshold Selection Method from Gray-Level Histograms." IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics. (介紹了 Otsu’s method)
Gonzalez, R. C., & Woods, R. E. (2018). "Digital Image Processing" (4th Edition). Pearson.（一本經典的數字圖像處理教科書）
Jain, A. K. (1989). "Fundamentals of Digital Image Processing." Prentice Hall.（包含大量圖像分割技術）
Canny, J. (1986). "A Computational Approach to Edge Detection." IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence. (Canny 邊緣檢測的原始論文)
